      • Professional Courses & Workshops

      • African School of Management Technology conducts Special Training Courses, Seminars and Workshop in Nigeria, within the ECOWAS Sub-Region and the African continent. The efforts of ASMT are made more successful and rewarding as a result of our belief in extensive and open minded collaboration with other institutions of higher learning such as renowned Universities, Polytechnics, Colleges and Professional Bodies of repute. We believe in bringing experts on each topic of our course, seminar or workshop discourse to ensure that participants gain adequately from such programmes. The environment where our programmes are conducted is another important consideration. We always ensure that participants are seated for classwork in very conducive environments. In most cases high ranking and well secured hotels are used to ensure that participants are saved the cost of travelling inconveniences from venues to their apartments.
