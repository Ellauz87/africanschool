Academic Programmes.

African School of Management Technologyhas designed academic programmes to suit the needs of full time students and part-time executive candidates on online and home tuition arrangements. The academic programmes of ASMT are run in collaboration with renowned professional bodies; the universities and other tertiary institutions offering globally recognized qualifications in affective and cognitive education. The School ensures that its students are qualified and eligible for admission and registration into the qualifying examinations relevant to their chosen career. The African School of Management Technology is involved in technologically-based education to grow the African economy with comparative abilities for competition and enhancement of self-sustaining and self-developing systems of productive economy.
