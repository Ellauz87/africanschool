about us

African School of Management Technology was established in 2007 to provide educational services in management, professional skills development and capacity building.  The School famously called ASMT has been in the vanguard of ensuring technologically equipped manpower with independent minded entrepreneurship skills.  The African School of Management Technology is determined to ensure that Africa and African youths have the comparative ability and skills to compete in global best practices in the arts, science and technology.

The Vision and Mission of ASMT are:

  • To build a professional institution with complete academic blend into professional expertise;

  • To produce the technologists and scientists including self-employed technicians and artisans that would drive self-sustaining and self-reliant economy with incorruptible leadership in African nations;

  • To contribute to global economic wellbeing through initiative, innovative, constructive production of goods and services based on improved copy engineering.
