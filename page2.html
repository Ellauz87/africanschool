      • Professional Post-Graduate Diploma (PPGD) Programmes

      • The professional post-graduate diploma(PPGD) is designed to promote the highest ethical standards and professional best practices within each chosen career. The qualification and award of diploma would be based on three-string approach namely;

        The successful completion and defense of project work (dissertation) on topical subject within the core career professional values;

        Successful completion of the final examination of the chosen professional body or the final examination of the Degree awarding University

        The successful completion of final examination of the African School of Management Technology as approved by the Academic Management and Advisory Council (AMAC) and Academic Assessors Panel (AAP) of the School.

      • Examinations and Qualifications

      • I. The primary concern of African School of Management Technology is to produce students and graduates who will become self-motivated and employable, skilled technicians, craftsmen and women controlling supervisory and middle level management strata of organizations on the one hand and top-notch self-employed, entrepreneurs and managers with leadership skills to drive self-reliant and self-sustaining economic growth and productivity.


        II. The examination certificates and diploma obtained through the African School of Management Technology are globally acceptable qualifications. We imbibe and treasure the values of reading and learning for knowledge in order to assume responsibility with authority and thereby acquire commensurate power to act in our certificate and diploma holders so that they can become the bride of excellence wherever they are found.


        III. The products (our students) are prepared with high quality standards to defend and attest to their world-class rating.


        IV. However, it must be noted that the accreditation and ranking of certificates is a role for regulatory bodies of Government Ministries, Departments and Agencies (MDAs) who must be cautious to ensure both equilibrium and compliance with relevant laws of the lands and districts within their jurisdiction. Those measures are not injurious to the profound competencies of certificate or diploma holders as every employer of labor is at liberty to choose what he/she recognizes and accepts for the organization's needs and purposes.

      • Employment and Service to Humanity

      • The School is not an employment agency but has the privilege of receiving requests for direct recruitment ofthose qualitative students required to fill in special vacancies in various organizations. The Management is usually interested within its resources and capacity to render all manner of services to humanity especially the host community and students of the School.

      • Social Responsibility

      • The African philosophy of being brothers' keeper ignites the urge for members of staff, students and Management to embark on social responsibility activities involving community service, supporting the needy, attending to social and religious obligations towards uplifting mankind.
